Epson ColorWorks C3500

The Epson ColorWorks™ C3500 Color Inkjet Label Printer with Just in Time Color™ labeling eliminates label waste and dramatically reduces the cost of label operations. It is ideal for high-mix, low-volume applications and features print speeds up to 4”/second, color-fast, smudge-proof printing with resolution up to 720 x 360 dpi and supports a wide variety of media types and sizes.

ColorWorks™ Color Label Printers:

Increase productivity and reduce the cost of using pre-printed labels with ColorWorks solutions, delivering dependable, commercial inkjet printing with fast-drying, durable color inks.

Label Printing Solutions From Epson

Feature Epson's award-winning MICRO PIEZO Print Head Technology and DURABrite Ultra Ink which provide extremely accurate ink disbursement, vibrant color and remarkably sharp text.
