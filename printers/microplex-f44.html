Microplex F44

Continous Form Laser Printer

Microplex Solid F44

The F44 printer offers a small footprint and provides the user with versatile and reliable media management capabilities, by utilizing the pin-fed tractor assembly and the optional Intelligent Paper Stacker (iPS).

  • • USPS MERLIN & Intelligent Mail® Barcode Compliant
  • • 44 Page Per Minute (8.5" Page Length), 34 Page Per Minute (11" Page Length)
  • • 4"-12" Media Widths, 11" (W) Max Print Area.
  • • 600 X 600 Dpi Print Resolution
  • • User-Friendly, Easy Setup and Easy to Operate
  • • Tear-off Edge for Separating of Printed Form Sets
