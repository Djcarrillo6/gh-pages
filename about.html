Why Choose ABA

ABA Moriah Corporation has been selling and servicing printers since 1984. ABA Moriah is one of Printronix / Tally largest nationwide value added resellers. ABA Moriah also sells and services Epson, Dascom/Tally, HP printers, Lexmark, Printek and is a Zebra Certified Premier Partner and Zebra Approved Service Partner (ZASP). With sales offices in Virginia, Texas and California and service nationwide, we are positioned to service the largest corporations and Federal agencies.

ABA e-Service Portal

eService Portal provides easy online access to service. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, the eService Portal enables you to submit call requests, track open service calls, manage service performance, and access service call histories in real time. Any ABA Moriah customer with a contract for onsite service or depot service can use the eService Portal. This service is ideal for IT or operations managers with multiple sites and equipment to oversee, and customers with large amounts of equipment under contract that they need to manage.

Our team of professional service technicians receive intensive training and education to quickly get your printers working properly. Our technicians will perform a print quality test upon completion of service to ensure your satisfaction. Excellent print quality and prompt repair service from ABA Moriah will keep your printers working for you. You simply call the toll free service number 800-288-1188 when an error code appears or when your printer is malfunctioning.

ABA CallTracker Features:

  • - Place service orders.
  • - Monitor service call status.
  • - View service call history, including details on the time the call was placed, technician dispatched, parts ordered and call resolution.
  • - View contracted assets by contract number.
  • - Centrally manage multiple locations, including asset inventory and tracking.
  • - Secure access with password protection.
  • - Establish multiple user accounts.
  • - Add comments or questions to open calls.
  • - Custom reOrder forms for easy ordering of supplies, print heads etc (Please talk to you sale executive to create a custom order form.)
