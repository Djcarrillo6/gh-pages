Multi-Vendor Service

Service & Support Specialists

Uniquely Positioned to Provide Multi-Vendor Printer Service, Help Desk Support and Product Refresh.

Global Profile

With locations throughout North America, ABA Moriah is one of the largest independent authorized and certified printer repair organizations in the US, servicing over 12,000 printers nationwide.

Service Model

ABA Moriah offers a variety of service and support options designed to meet the customer's individual requirements and expectations. Service levels include: contracted parts and labor repair, on demand time and material repair, and depot repair options.

Certified Staff

Service and Support are managed by experienced and highly trained Support Center Technicians from start to finish. When onsite response is required, Field Technicians are dispatched and supported every step of the way by the Support Center.

Integrated CRM Platform

ABA Moriah CRM software links all customer account information and connects all Support Center Staff and Field Technicians with complete online, real-time account history and status. From start to finish all facets of service and support are recorded and managed in CRM, including call receipt and triage, Field Technician and parts dispatch, Call Center support for Field Technicians and spare parts retrieval.

eService Web Portal

Service Call information is available to the contract maintenance holder through a password protected online web portal. Authorized users can place calls, track call status, monitor service performance, manage assets and review call history.

Regional Support Centers

Our three support and parts distribution centers in Washington, DC, Dallas, TX and Orange County, CA are connected by ABA's CRM software enabling our staff to respond with call support and spare parts dispatch from East and West Coast time zones.

Multi-Vendor Product Support

With over 30 years of experience servicing the printer marketplace exclusively, we have compiled an impressive knowledge base and listing of supported products. We are partnered and certified with most of the industry's leading manufacturers with direct access to their technical resources and OEM spare parts.
